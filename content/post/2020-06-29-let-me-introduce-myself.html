Hi, I’m Dan. I’m a pediatric resident with the IU School of Medicine in Indianapolis, which means I spend most of my time at the awesome Riley Hospital for Children. I spent my last month in our intensive care unit, which was a humbling experience in a few different ways. First of all in terms of how much medicine I still have to learn after 6+ years of education/training. Also, in terms of the awesome power we healthcare providers have to sustain our patients’ lives. But finally, how limited we still are to alter the overall life course of most children.
